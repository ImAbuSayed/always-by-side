[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel your heartbeat through the rhythm of the night
Shadows dancing softly in the fading candlelight
Every time you look at me, I leave the world behind
I just want to hold you and be always by your side

[Verse 1]
We got two glasses empty and a fire burning slow
There is nowhere else on earth that I would rather go
Your fingers trace my shoulder and it takes my breath away
I could listen to your silence for the rest of my days

[Pre-Chorus]
The hour is getting late but we are only starting out
You are the only certainty I have inside the doubt
I lean a little closer just to whisper in your ear
Everything I ever wanted is sitting right here

[Chorus]
You are my midnight drive down a country road
You are the sweetest secret that my heart could hold
I want to fall into the velvet of your eyes
Wrap you in my arms and be always by your side

[Post-Chorus]
Always by your side, making every moment right
(Always by your side, through the middle of the night)

[Verse 2]
I love the way your hair falls down across your face
Every single touch of yours is a beautiful escape
We do not need a reason to stay tangled in these sheets
You are the perfect melody that makes my soul complete

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The world is spinning fast but we are moving nice and slow
I will follow you tonight wherever you want to go
I pull you in a little tighter, kiss your collarbone
As long as I am with you, I will always be at home

[Chorus]
You are my midnight drive down a country road
You are the sweetest secret that my heart could hold
I want to fall into the velvet of your eyes
Wrap you in my arms and be always by your side

[Post-Chorus 2]
Always by your side, making every moment right
(Always by your side, through the middle of the night)

[Bridge]
Every broken road I walked has led me right to this
Trading all the empty nights for just a single kiss
I never thought a simple touch could make me lose control
But you got Saeed falling, surrendering it all
There is nothing in the universe that I would ever trade
For the perfect little paradise that you and I have made

[Spoken]
Look into my eyes, I am never letting you go
You are the only truth my heart will ever know

[Final Chorus]
You are my midnight drive down a country road
You are the sweetest secret that my heart could hold
I want to fall into the velvet of your eyes
Wrap you in my arms and be always by your side
